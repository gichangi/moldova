General objective no. 2. Ensuring adults and children have zero tolerance for any form of violence against children, so that the share of children subjected to any form of violence decrease from 1.6% in 2020 to 0.5% in 2026, and the share of population believing that any act of violence against a child has to be reported to the authorities, calling thus for their intervention, increase from 85.6% in 2018 to 100% in 2026.
